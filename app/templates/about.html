Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No! We welcome people with all skill levels. We provide training and mentoring for all new team members.

How much time commitment is required?

Our team members typically serve on Sundays and occasionally for special events. We're flexible with scheduling to accommodate different availability.

What is the application process?

Fill out our application form, and we'll review your portfolio and interests. Then you'll go through three phases: Portfolio Review, Shadow Service, and a Practical Test.

Can I apply for multiple subunits?

Yes! However, we ask you to select your primary interest on the application. You can discuss additional roles once you're on the team.
